Joozy's Web3 music features allow users to support their favorite artists directly by purchasing NFTs that represent unique rights to their music, accessing exclusive content, and participating in decentralized music streaming services. Joozy also offers advanced music discovery features, including personalized recommendations based on user preferences and data analysis of decentralized music streams.

The Joozy platform is built on Solana. The Joozy group is a group of Devs who participated in the Solana Hackathon in 2023 and also the Tron-Hackathon in 2022.
